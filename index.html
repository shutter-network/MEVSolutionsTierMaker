What is this?

Maximal Extractable Value (MEV) represents the maximum value that can be extracted from block production in excess of the standard block reward and gas fees. This tier maker helps you rank different solutions that protect users from malicious MEV attacks.

Drag and drop the MEV protection solutions into tiers. S-tier represents the most effective solutions, while D-tier represents the least effective ones.
